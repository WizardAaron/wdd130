Raging Rapids Expedition

Hold on tight—this heart-pounding ride is not for the faint of heart. The Raging Rapids Expedition throws you into a wild stretch of Class IV–V rapids, where churning whitewater and sudden drops test your courage and teamwork. Ideal for seasoned rafters, this expedition rewards thrill-seekers with jaw-dropping canyon views, surprise splashes, and an unforgettable adrenaline rush.

Shadow Canyon Run

Glide through towering cliffs and emerald shadows on this scenic, half-day adventure. Shadow Canyon Run offers a perfect blend of serenity and excitement, with moderate Class III rapids nestled between ancient rock formations and peaceful bends. Great for families and first-time rafters, it’s a soulful journey through one of the region’s most hauntingly beautiful landscapes.

The Whirlpool Rush

Get swept up—literally—in the swirling chaos of The Whirlpool Rush. Known for its unpredictable eddies and powerful currents, this trip offers a dynamic ride that’s equal parts challenging and exhilarating. Surrounded by misty hillsides and echoing roars, you’ll navigate a natural obstacle course of twists, turns, and splash zones that keep you laughing (and maybe screaming) from start to finish.
